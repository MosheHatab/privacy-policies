Privacy Policy

Mechanical Keyboard Simulator

Last updated: February 15, 2026

HIE-YA ("we", "us", "our") built the Mechanical Keyboard Simulator app ("the App") as a free, ad-supported app. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Device identifier (Google Play Games ID or anonymous device ID)
  • Gameplay data: key clicks, play time, session count, level, XP, coins, unlocked items
  • Age category (13+ or under 13) — used solely for COPPA-compliant ad targeting
  • No personal information (name, email, phone) is collected
This App does not sell user data to third parties.

2. How We Use Information

  • Cloud save: sync your game progress across sessions
  • Leaderboards: display anonymous rankings
  • Analytics: improve the app experience
  • Ad targeting: serve age-appropriate ads

3. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

The App includes an age gate. Users who indicate they are under 13 receive only contextual, non-personalized ads. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. No behavioral tracking is performed for child users.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — serves banner, interstitial, and rewarded ads (Privacy Policy)
  • Google Play Games Services — anonymous sign-in for leaderboards and cloud save (Privacy Policy)
  • Amazon Web Services (AWS) — stores gameplay data via DynamoDB, Lambda, and API Gateway (Privacy Policy)

5. Data Storage and Security

Data is stored on AWS servers in the EU (Frankfurt, eu-central-1). All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Game data is retained as long as you use the App. To request deletion of your data, contact us at moshe.hatab.projects@gmail.com. We will delete your data within 30 days.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
